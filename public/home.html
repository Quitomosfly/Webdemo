How It Works

                    
Our system allows you to easily schedule events with flexibility and precision. Here's how it works:

                    
    
                        
  • Choose between scheduling by specific dates or recurring days of the week.
    • 
                        
  • Define the time ranges that suit your needs.
    • 
                        
  • Submit your event, and our system will handle availability coordination.
    • 
                        
  • Participants can select their preferred time slots based on your settings.
    • 
                    

                    
This approach ensures that scheduling conflicts are minimized and that everyone involved can participate at their convenience.

                

                    
                
                

                    
                    

                        
Q: Can I select multiple dates? 
 A: Yes, you can choose multiple dates in the date picker.

                        
Q: What if my event spans different time slots? 
 A: You can define a time range that works for you.
